About Us


Established in 1970, Huntington Park Auto Center Inc stands as your reliable hub for automotive body repair solutions in Topeka, KS. We take pride in delivering premium services, with a focus on auto body repairs, collision repair, custom auto paint, dent removals, and frame work. Our dedication to maintaining high standards has positioned us as a dependable option for individuals in search of superior automotive solutions. We extend our services to cater to the needs of both English and Spanish-speaking clients, emphasizing effective communication and comprehension.
